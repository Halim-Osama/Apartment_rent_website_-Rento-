About Us

I am a student from the Egypt-Japan University of Science and Technology (E-JUST), and the idea for this project came from my own real struggles while looking for apartments. I dealt with brokers who gave me misleading information, exaggerated details, and sometimes completely changed the reality of the apartment. After going through this several times, I decided to build something better — a platform where people can find honest, clear, and reliable listings without hassle.

My goal is simple: make the process of finding a rental apartment straightforward and transparent.

I designed this website so owners can easily upload their apartments with real details and photos, and so renters can explore places based on their location, budget, and needs — without worrying about false information.

I believe everyone deserves to search for a home with confidence, not stress. This platform is my attempt to solve a problem I experienced ourselves, and hopefully make things easier for many others.

Whether you're looking to list your apartment or searching for your next home, I'm here to help and make the experience smoother for you.
